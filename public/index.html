Quick Gardening Tips 🌿

💧 Water Smart

Water plants in the morning to keep roots hydrated throughout the day.

☀️ Light Matters

Rotate pots weekly so all sides get equal sunlight exposure.

♻️ Reuse Containers

Old jars, bottles, or tins make great eco-friendly plant pots.

🌱 Feed the Soil

Healthy soil means healthy plants—add compost regularly.
