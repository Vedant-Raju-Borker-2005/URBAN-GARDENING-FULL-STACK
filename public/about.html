🌱 Our Mission

At The Urban Planting, our mission is to make gardening accessible and enjoyable for everyone, no matter the size of their home or the limitations of city living. We believe that plants do more than just decorate—they bring life, calmness, and a sense of purpose to our daily routines. A single green corner can transform the atmosphere of a home, promote healthier lifestyles, and remind us to live in harmony with nature even in the busiest urban environments.

Through easy-to-follow guides, practical tips, and eco-friendly practices, we aim to empower individuals to create thriving green spaces on balconies, rooftops, windowsills, or any small area they can nurture. Our focus is not only on helping plants grow but also on building a culture of sustainability, mindfulness, and community. By inspiring more people to embrace gardening, we envision greener cities where nature and urban life coexist beautifully, one plant at a time.

Meet the Creators

SWAYAM PRABHU

A passionate urban gardener who finds peace in transforming the smallest of spaces into vibrant green corners. From balconies and windowsills to rooftops, he have mastered the art of bringing nature into the heart of the city. With a strong belief that gardening is not limited by space, he dedicates his time to teaching city dwellers how to reconnect with nature through simple and sustainable practices. Their work focuses on creative planting solutions, eco-friendly methods, and inspiring others to find joy, relaxation, and wellness in nurturing plants.

VEDANT BORKER

A dedicated plant enthusiast and educator who firmly believes that greenery has the power to heal, inspire, and bring people closer to nature. With a deep passion for photography, he focuses on making gardening approachable and enjoyable for beginners, breaking down complex concepts into simple, practical steps. His philosophy emphasizes that anyone can nurture plants, regardless of prior experience, and that each small effort contributes to a healthier environment.
